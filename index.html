How each branch Works together

Checks
And Balances

The 3 branches work together through the system checks and balances. In the Legislative branch the Senate and the House proposes and debates laws. The executive branch, has the power to sign bills or veto laws. The judicial branch assesses if the laws are constitutional.

My favorite branch

The judicial branch is my personal my favorite, through its profound impact on justice in all of it's cases. Take Plessy v. Ferguson."In reaching this conclusion he relied on the Supreme Court’s ruling in the Civil Rights Cases (1883), which found that racial discrimination against African Americans in inns, public conveyances, and places of public amusement imposes no badge of slavery or involuntary servitude…but at most, infringes rights which are protected from State aggression by the XIVth Amendment."(Brian Duignan) An amazing example where the Supreme Court's decision changed the course of civil rights. Cases like Brown v. Board of Education and Roe v. Wade further prove the judicial branch's role in changing societal norms and enforces the laws that are passed.
